Choosing a provider often comes down to trust, clarity, and fit.

## Useful questions to ask

- How do you approach intake and service matching?
- What types of needs are you experienced supporting?
- How do you communicate with families or guardians?
- What should we expect after submitting a form or referral?
- How do you set expectations around timelines and availability?

## Why these questions matter

They help families spot clear, realistic communication.
