Intake is easier when the basics are ready.

## Helpful details to gather

- the individual's age group and current routines
- for Day Program inquiries, whether the person needing support is an adult 18+
- region or city where support is needed
- the service you are exploring
- preferred days, times, or urgency level
- communication preferences and support considerations
- known safety, behavioural, or mobility needs

## Helpful questions to answer as a family

- What outcome matters most right now?
- What kind of support schedule feels realistic?
- What would make the service feel successful after the first few months?

Preparation makes intake clearer.
