Respite works best when it is planned with care.

## What structured respite planning can include

- routines that should stay consistent
- activities or transitions that need extra support
- communication preferences
- medication, mobility, or risk-awareness considerations
- the caregiver outcomes that matter most

## A good respite plan supports the whole household

Families often need more than occasional coverage. They need support that reduces decision fatigue.
